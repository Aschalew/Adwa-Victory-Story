Privacy Policy — Adwa Victory

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: The Adwa Victory app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account is required. No internet connection is needed.

1. Information We Collect

The Adwa Victory application does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Data Storage

The app stores only your quiz score locally on your device using Android's standard application storage. This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app. We have no access to this data.

3. Internet Access

The Adwa Victory app does not require an internet connection to function. All content (historical text, timeline, quiz questions) is stored locally within the app. The app does not make any network requests and does not communicate with any servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The Adwa Victory app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No third-party SDKs are included in the app.

5. Children's Privacy

The Adwa Victory app is suitable for all ages including children. Since we collect no personal data whatsoever, the app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent international regulations.

6. Permissions

The Adwa Victory app does not request any special Android permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any other device features beyond what is required to display the app on screen.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the Adwa Victory app, please contact us at: adwavictoryapp@gmail.com
